The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world and stands at a height of 330 meters. It was designed by the French engineer Gustave Eiffel and serves as a global symbol of France. The tower attracts millions of visitors every year and offers breathtaking views of Paris from its observation decks. Originally constructed as the entrance arch for the 1889 World's Fair, it was initially criticized but has since become a beloved architectural masterpiece.
